About Palestine Rising

Palestine Rising is dedicated to raising awareness about the struggles and triumphs of the Palestinian people. Our mission is to provide a platform for voices advocating for justice and freedom.

Our Vision

We envision a world where the rights and dignity of the Palestinian people are recognized and respected. Through education, advocacy, and community engagement, we strive to make this vision a reality.
